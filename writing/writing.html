Writing

I tell stories. Always have. As a kid, playtime frequently involved a story. My friends and I never just played catch, we were competing in the championship game. Bike rides around the neighborhood were epic quests to find lost artifacts and to defeat the monsters. I would run through the woods behind our house with a wooden sword, attacking giants (trees). I have found that mundane tasks get a little more bearable when framed inside a larger story.

I also believe in the power of the essay, of writing on a topic, getting thoughts down on paper. This is why I started my first blog, just to put my opinions on various topics out into the world. I hope to continue this here, but the main focus will be on my fiction work.

I may not be the most knowledgeable about things like keys and modes and music theory, but I have listened to a lot of music in my life. This section will be all about my experiences at live shows, album reviews, my own questionable attempts at crafting music, and just music in general.
